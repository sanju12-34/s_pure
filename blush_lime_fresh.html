Blush Lime Fresh

Blush Lime Fresh

Available in 75 Gram And 120 Gram

Following a taxing and stress-laden day, each individual merits a rejuvenating bath capable of dispelling all accumulated stress and weariness from their mind. Our lime-infused soap offers precisely such a revitalizing experience. 

Ingredients  :

Our exceptional lime soap boasts a composition enriched with extraordinary natural components. It includes the essence of pure lime essential oil, the extract of zesty lime, and a blend of remarkable herbs, all contributing to astonishing skin benefits.

The essential oil within our soap offers numerous advantages. Its inherent natural antibacterial properties safeguard and fortify the skin. The infusion of Vitamin C bestows a radiant luminosity upon your complexion. Furthermore, it gently exfoliates, ridding the skin of lifeless cells. The lime extract imparts a splendid aroma, creating a soothing and stress-relieving ambiance. Our soap not only leaves you thoroughly cleansed but also deeply nourished, preserving the skin’s inherent moisture.

  • Blush Lime Fresh Soap: A revitalizing blend of freshly squeezed limes for an energizing burst of citrus fragrance.
  • Vitamin C-Rich: Abundant in natural lime extracts, providing potent antioxidants for youthful, radiant skin.
  • Gentle Cleansing: Creates a luxurious lather, effectively removing impurities without stripping natural moisture.
  • All Skin Types: Specially formulated for sensitivity, free from harsh chemicals and artificial additives.
  • Begin by moistening your skin with a gentle splash of water.
  • Apply the soap delicately to generate a foamy lather.
  • Gently massage for 30 seconds.
  • Thoroughly rinse with water.
  • Pat dry using a microfiber towel.
  • For soap preservation, store in a clean, dry location to prevent melting.
