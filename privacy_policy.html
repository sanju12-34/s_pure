Privacy Policy For S Pure

At S Pure, accessible from https://s-pure.com, ensuring the privacy of our visitors is a top priority for us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we utilize it. 

Our Privacy Policy is designed to inform visitors about how we handle the information they share with us online. Please note that this policy specifically pertains to our online activities and does not cover information collected through offline means or channels other than this website. 

CONSENT 

By engaging with our website, you are acknowledging your consent to abide by our Privacy Policy and its terms. 

INFORMATION WE COLLECT 

You will be informed of the specific personal information we require and the purpose behind it will be made clear to you.  \ If you contact us directly, we may gather further details about you, including your name, email address, phone number, as well as the content of your message and any attachments you send, along with any other information you decide to share with us.  When creating an account, we may request your contact details, such as your name, company name, address, email address, and telephone number. 

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION 

We utilize the information we gather for a variety of purposes, such as:

  • Ensuring the smooth operation and maintenance of our website.
  • Enhancing and personalizing your experience on our platform. Gaining insights into how you interact with our website.
  • Gaining insights into how you interact with our website.
  • Innovating and introducing new products, services, and features.
  • Engaging with you directly or through our partners for customer support, updates, and relevant information about the website, as well as for marketing efforts.
  • Keeping you informed through emails
  • Identifying and preventing fraudulent activities.

LOG FILES 

At S Pure, we adhere to industry-standard practices by utilizing log files. These files record the details of visitors when they access our website. This is a common practice among hosting companies and is an integral part of hosting services’ analytics. The information captured by these log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISPs), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and potentially the number of clicks. It’s important to note that this data is not linked to any personally identifiable information. The primary purpose of collecting this information is to analyse trends, manage the site, monitor users’ navigation patterns, and gather demographic insights. 

COOKIES AND WEB BEACONS 

S Pure, like many other websites, utilizes ‘cookies’ to enhance the user experience. These cookies store information about visitors’ preferences and the pages they access. This data helps us tailor our website content to better suit the user’s browsing preferences, optimizing their overall experience. 

Advertising Partners Privacy Policies 

You can refer to this list to access the Privacy Policies of our advertising partners at S Pure.

Our website may display advertisements and links from third-party ad servers or ad networks. These entities may utilize technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons to deliver their respective ads. When this happens, your IP address is automatically collected. 

These technologies serve to gauge the success of their advertising efforts and customize the advertising content you encounter on other websites. 

Please be aware that S Pure does not have the ability to access or manage these cookies employed by third-party advertisers. 

Third Party Privacy Policies 

S Pure’s Privacy Policy specifically pertains to our own practices and does not extend to other advertisers or websites. We recommend reviewing the Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for comprehensive information on their practices and guidance on opting out of certain options. 

If you wish, you can opt to disable cookies by adjusting your browser settings. For further guidance on managing cookies with specific web browsers, please refer to the respective browser’s official website. 

GDPR  - General Data Protection Rights 

We want to ensure that you are fully aware of your data protection rights. As a user, you have the following entitlements: 

THE RIGHT TO ACCESS : You can request copies of your data. Please note that a small fee may apply for this service. 

THE RIGHT TO RECTIFICATION : If you believe any information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections. 

THE RIGHT TO ERASURE : Under certain conditions, you can request that we delete your data. 

THE RIGHT TO RESTRICT PROCESSING :Under certain conditions, you can request restrictions on the processing of your data. 

THE RIGHT TO OBJECT TO PROCESSING : Under certain conditions, you can object to how we process your data. 

THE RIGHT TO DATA PORTABILITY : Under certain conditions, you can request that we transfer the data we’ve collected to another organization or directly to you. 

We will respond to your request within one month. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us. 

Children’s Information 

Ensuring the safety of children while they’re online, is a top priority for us. 

We urge parents and guardians to be actively involved in overseeing and guiding their online activities. 

Rest assured, S Pure does not intentionally gather any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has inadvertently provided such information on our website, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us. 

We are committed to promptly addressing this and taking the necessary steps to remove any such information from our records. 

Changes to This Privacy Policy 

We may occasionally update our Privacy Policy, so we recommend checking this page regularly for any revisions. Any changes will be promptly communicated by posting the updated Privacy Policy here. 

These modifications will take effect immediately upon being posted. 

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to get in touch with us.
